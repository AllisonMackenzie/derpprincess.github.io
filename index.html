Full Stack Web Developer

I started my journey as a Software Engineer in 2014 by enrolling in the Computer Science program at the University of Pikeville.

It was there that I learned the fundamentals of Software Design, Data Structures & Algorithms, and Object Oriented Programming.

I was primarily exposed to Java, with some basic introductory to Python, Swift, Racket, Prolog, and C.

At university, I worked as an Academic Tutor teaching Computer Science and English in 2017 before embarking on my study abroad to Nagoya, Japan where I attended NUFS!

I made some of my own side projects and worked on some great open source projects like OpenMRS!

May 2019, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Since then, I expanded my skills to include C#, JavaScript, and TypeScript.

Because of the COVID Pandemic, I spent the better portion of 2020 improving my skills and learning new ones such as Web Development with React.

I started freelancing in 2020, making websites for people using technology such as React and WordPress. Some websites I've made are even Full Stack MERN Apps!

This is my portfolio.
